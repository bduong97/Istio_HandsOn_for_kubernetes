- Kiali puts a special label to pods labled with "app: <value>"; Helpful to stick to this notation when labeling pods for services
- Virtual services are not replacements for services; Services are for k8s service discovery, VirtualServices are used for overriding the envoy proxies configuration in order to do stuff like traffic routing
- Consistent hashing doesn't work with weighted routing; dont try it
